Nengo Hill Camping


Nengo Hill offers an unparalleled camping experience where adventure and serenity coalesce. This exclusive retreat allows guests to immerse themselves in nature's embrace without sacrificing the comforts of home. Your campsite, perched atop the undulating hills, awaits your arrival, complete with a welcoming fireplace to dispel the evening chill. Whether in search of solitude or a convivial gathering with dear ones, our facilities guarantee an indelible sojourn. At Nengo Hill, we pledge to make your ideal escape a reality. Secure your spot today and elevate your camping experience to extraordinary.


The campsite's location crowns the summit of Nengo Hill in Gisenyi City, within Rwanda's western province. Its closeness to the shores of Lake Kivu positions it as a prime camping locale.


The scenery afforded by Nengo Hill is nothing short of breathtaking. To one side, the sunset over Lake Kivu presents a tableau of glittering waters reaching for the horizon. To the opposite, the sunrise amidst the neighboring hills offers a symphony of birdsong complemented by the aroma of morning coffee from the photo platform.


Embark on an adventure along the Nengo nature trail and discover the serenity of its cozy camping site. Capture the beauty of the great outdoors with your camera, and find a moment of peace to reconnect with yourself amidst the natural splendor. Should you have any questions or require additional information, please do not hesitate to reach out.
