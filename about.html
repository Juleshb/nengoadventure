About Nengo Hill Adventure

The Nengo Hill Adventure is one of Kivu Expeditions Ltd managed tourism products, the developement of the project started in early 2020. Currently, only the first phase is operational, this includes, the hiking trail, a view point platform, a mountain swing and a cozy camping site with a fireplace.

About Kivu Expeditions Ltd

Kivu Expeditions Ltd has been a significant player in the tourism development of East Africa since 2010, engaging in extensive tourism research, product development, tour planning and CBT development.
