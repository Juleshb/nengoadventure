Nengo Hiking Trail

A fascinating place to explore. Here are some additional details about the trail:

  • Location: : Nengo Hill Trail is located in Gisenyi City, Rwanda’s western province. The proximity to Lake Kivu and the border with the Democratic Republic of Congo makes it an ideal spot for hikers seeking both natural beauty and historical significance.
  • Difficulty: The moderate rating is accurate. Expect some challenging sections due to steep terrain and rocky paths.
  • Duration: The 4.1 km length of the trail allows for a leisurely exploration. Completing the trail takes approximately 3 to 4 hours.
  • Scenery: The panoramic vistas from lookout photo platform are truly captivating. On one side, you’ll enjoy views of Lake Kivu. On the other side, the surrounding mountains create a dramatic backdrop.
  • Historical Significance: Nengo Hill’s past as a German military strategic defense site during World War I adds depth to the experience.
  • Cultural insights: Nengo Hill is surrounded by vibrant communities, especially women, who offer memorable community based tourism activities.

Come and explore Nengo Trail, take plenty of photos, and immerse yourself in its unique blend of nature and history while meeting the welcoming people of Gisenyi and share a smile. If you have any enquiries or need further information, feel free to contact us!
